Every problem should be solved only once

Blue Dot is a software studio in Turin building AI for business process automation — for the web and the app stores. We make software that gives people their time back, reduces errors, and lets work run cleaner.

The name

Blue Dot is named for the Pale Blue Dot — Earth photographed by Voyager 1 from six billion kilometres away, and Carl Sagan's reflection on that image. Seen from far enough out, everything we do, large or small, collapses into a single point of light.

“a mote of dust suspended in a sunbeam”Carl Sagan

We take that distance as a kind of clarity about what actually matters. Our aim is to be more human through technology — to hand off to machines everything that pulls us apart, and keep for ourselves what makes us human: being present, thoughtful, gentle, caring, together. We're building toward a world full of technology where people come out freer because of it.

What we do

We work in two modes. We ship commercial products as SaaS, and we run our own research into how frontier AI models fit into real business systems. The two feed each other: research sharpens the products, and the products keep the research honest.
